How to use

Create an Appointment

  • Click on the "Book an Appointment" link on top right corner
  • Add your name and purpose of your appointment
  • Choose your preferred date and time for the appointment using the date and time picker.
  • Click on Save

Created Appointments

  • You will be able to view successfully created appoints in the table beside the form
  • Created appointments will have 2 action buttons. 1. Edit and 2. Delete
  • When you click on Edit button your appointment data will be transfered to the form and changes can be made and saved
  • When you click on Delete button, a pop up will appear for you to confirm your action

Some of the functionality

  • Easy to use UI
  • Current time and date for a quick look
  • Form Validation
  • Ability to Edit and Delete created appointments
  • Shows an confirm pop up for you to confirm when you click delete an appointment
  • Warns you about data loss when you reload or try to leave the page
